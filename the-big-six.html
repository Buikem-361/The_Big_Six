THE PREM'S ELITE

  • Liverpool

    This football club has won the prem 20 times with the most recent being under
    their new manager Arne Slot. They have also enjoyed thier most recent
    European glory in 2020 under Jurgen Klopp when they lifted club football's
    2nd biggest trophy in the UEFA Champions League. The club and its manager
    Arne Slot are under a little bit of pressure due to thier most recent dip in form
    with the likes of Mo Salah and Florian Wirtz failing to live up to the fan's
    expectations.

    Trophies Won since 2020

    1. Premier League (2019/20, 2024/25)
    2. UEFA Super Cup (2019/20)
    3. FIFA Club World Cup (2019/20)
    4. FA Cup (2021/22)
    5. League Cup (2021/22, 2023/24)
    6. FA Community Shield (2022)
